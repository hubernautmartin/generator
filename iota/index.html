A seed represented as random words is safe. For a brute-force attacker such a seed is just as difficult to guess as the fully random 81 character IOTA seed. Also, words are much easier to write down or even remember. Though it's best to write them down or print them and put them in a safe place even if you have a great memory, just in case. Trusted bitcoin wallets such as the Electrum bitcoin client use wallet words as well.
